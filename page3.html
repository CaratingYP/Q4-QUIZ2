Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are terraces that were carved into the mountains of Banaue, Ifugao, in the Philippines, by the ancestors of the Igorot people. The terraces are occasionally called the "Eighth Wonder of the World". It is commonly thought that the terraces were built with minimal equipment, largely by hand.These are some of the activities available in Banaue.

Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills are a rock formation in the Philippines' Bohol Province. There are at least 1,260 hills (possibly more) spread across more than 50 square kilometers (20 square miles).
